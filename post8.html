Happy Internsgiving!

A big thank you to my support team

Posted by Myranda on November 29, 2019
Share

Happy Thanksgiving from the interns at Glenn! Although we’re away from our families, we celebrated our NASA family together with Internsgiving this holiday.

When you’re surrounded in an environment with like-minded people, it’s easy to forget how special an opportunity you have. It’s tempting for me to let working at NASA become the new “normal.” I hope I never lose my gratitude for the position I have here.

In addition, I am incredibly thankful for every single reader and supporter of my internship this semester. Thank you for giving me the confidence to pursue my dreams and the inspiration to document them!
